About NUSHDev

NUSHDev is a student-led initiative at NUS High School Of Maths and Science, dedicated to website development. We aim in creating impactful digital solutions for our school community and beyond.

Led by Junying, we aim to create useful applications to the school community. From creating open source websites and applications to ethical hacking.

What We Do

Custom Web Tools

We build sleek, fast, and interactive class and student portals tailored specifically for NUSH. Our tools streamline daily operations and enhance accessibility for the entire school community.

Ethical Hacking & Security

Our team explores security vulnerabilities and conducts penetration testing to improve digital safety. We operate ethically to identify and mitigate risks, contributing to a more secure online environment for all.

Mobile & Desktop Apps

From essential task planners to engaging recreational games (INSPIRED BY aura clicker), NUSHDev delivers intuitive applications.
